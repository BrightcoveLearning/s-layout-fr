Introduction

App settings are configuration options that apply throughout your apps. To view the app settings navigate to the Layout module and click the gear icon to the right of the Layout module name.

Once the gear icon is clicked you see the full list of app settings, and how they are broken into the following three groups:

  • Display
  • Pages
  • Assets
