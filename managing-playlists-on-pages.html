All directions below assume you are on the page details page when starting. To get to the location, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. Click on a page name to open the page details.

Also note that layout options are slightly different depending on the layout chosen for the playlist. The options shown in the document are for carousels, but are slightly different than those for grids.

Add playlists

Video content is added to pages by adding them to playlists and then adding playlists to a page. A playlist is a collection of your video content, including videos, series, seasons, live events and channels. Before you add videos to pages, you should have videos organized into playlists.

  1. Click Add Playlist. A list of playlists that have been created displays in a panel.
  2. Select one or more playlists to add to the page by hovering over the playlist and then clicking Add.
    Note: The number of playlists that can be added is based upon the Page Type that was selected. Some Page Types only allow one playlist to be added.
  3. Click X in the panel to close the list of playlists.

Remove playlists

There are two ways to remove a playlist from a page:

  1. From the same panel you used to add playlists to page, hover over a playlist that is currently on the page and click Remove.
  2. Open the playlist settings dialog by clicking the ellipsis (...) next to the playlist, then click Remove Playlist.

Replace playlists

Replace a playlist following these steps:

  1. Open the playlist settings dialog by clicking the ellipsis (...) next to the playlist you wish to replace.
  2. Click Replace Playlist.
  3. The playlist panel opens, and you then choose which playlist to replace the selected one.

Change playlist orientation

Playlist orientation controls how the video images appear on the page. To change the playlist orientation, follow these steps.

  1. Open the playlist settings dialog by clicking the ellipsis (...) next to the playlist, then select an orientation, either Landscape or Portrait.
    • Landscape - Landscape images are displayed on the page
    • Portrait - Portrait images are displayed on the page

After selecting an orientation, the page preview updates.

Change playlist layout

You have a number of choices to how your playlist can be presented in the {{ site.product }} apps. To alter the layout, follow these steps:

  1. Open the playlist settings dialog by clicking the ellipsis (...) next to the playlist.
  2. From the playlist settings dialog, click More layout options.
  3. You see the playlist layout dialog.
  4. From this dialog you can choose to (the page preview updates to show your selections):
    • Change playlist orientation (just as you can from the playlist settings dialog).
    • Choose to show, or not show, video titles.
    • If you choose to show video titles, you can have them appear inside or outside the video thumbnail.

Edit playlist

If you wish to change the videos included in the playlist you can choose the Edit playlist option. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the playlist settings dialog by clicking the ellipsis (...) next to the playlist you wish to edit.
  2. Click Edit playlist.
  3. Edit the playlist in the new tab that opens to the Media module with your playlist open.
