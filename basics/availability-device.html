You can configure pages to appear on only specific devices. To configure the permitted/denied devices for a page, follow these steps:

When you make changes to the settings, a green circle with a checkmark animates to indicate your changes are automatically saved.
  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, hover over the page name, and click .
  3. In the Page Settings dialog, click Availability.
  4. In the far right of the Device restrictions row, click the toggle button.
  5. Check the boxes to allow viewing of a page from a group or individual device.
You can also configure device restrictions by clicking on a page name. From the page details, in the Availability section, click .
