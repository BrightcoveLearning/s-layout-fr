You can configure pages to appear in only specific locations. To configure permitted/denied locations, follow these steps:

When you make changes to the settings, a green circle with a checkmark animates to indicate your changes are automatically saved.
  1. Open the Layout module.
  2. In the side navigation panel, hover over a page name, and click .
  3. In the Page Settings dialog, click Availability.
  4. In the far right of the Location restrictions row, click the toggle button.
  5. Select the locations for both the desired permitted and denied locations by typing the name of a country/location. As you begin typing the list will filter.
You can also change the schedule by clicking on a page name. From the page details, in the Availability section, click .
